Got Questions?

FAQ — Furniture Assembly

Do I need to buy the furniture first?
+
Yes — we assemble what you already own or have received. We don't supply furniture, but we can assemble anything from any brand.
Can you do multiple pieces in one visit?
+
Absolutely — this is the most cost-effective way. Most clients have us knock out 3–6 items in a single visit. Send us the full list and we'll estimate time.
Do you dispose of the packaging?
+
Yes. We break down all cardboard and remove all packaging by default. Just let us know if you want to keep it.
What if a part is missing or broken?
+
We identify it on-site and help you contact the manufacturer for a replacement part. We don't charge for the time spent waiting if you need to pause.
How long does assembly take per item?
+
Simple items: 20–40 min. Large wardrobes or complex beds: 60–90 min. We'll give you a realistic estimate before arriving.
Can you disassemble furniture for moving?
+
Yes — disassembly is typically faster than assembly and priced by the hour at $70/hr.
