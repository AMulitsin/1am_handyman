📌 How Our Pricing Works

All prices assume standard conditions — replacing old with new, no custom modifications required. We estimate upfront; if something unexpected comes up mid-job, we talk before continuing. You pay for skill and speed, not the clock. For larger projects, we provide a free estimate.

Permit Note: Some minor electrical and plumbing work may require a city permit under California law. It is the homeowner's responsibility to obtain applicable permits. We are happy to advise on what may be required for your project.

All prices are starting estimates only. Final pricing is determined after a free on-site evaluation. Every home and project is different — actual cost depends on scope, accessibility, existing conditions, and materials. We never start work without your approval.

Got Questions?

FAQ

How much does it cost just to come out and look?
+
Nothing. The estimate visit is completely free with no obligation. We come out, assess the job, give you a clear price — you decide if you want to move forward.
What if the job ends up being harder than expected?
+
If something unexpected comes up after we start, we stop and talk to you first — always. We don't add charges without your knowledge. If the extra work is minor, we typically absorb it. If significant, we discuss openly before continuing.
Do I need to buy materials, or do you handle that?
+
Both options work. For small items (outlets, fixtures, hardware) we typically carry common parts. For larger items we'll give you a recommendation on exactly what to buy — or we can source it at cost, no markup.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
+
Simple — we come back and make it right at no charge. We offer a 30-day workmanship guarantee on all jobs. Our reputation is built on long-term relationships, not one-off transactions.
How quickly can you come out?
+
For most jobs we can do a free estimate within 24–48 hours. For urgent situations (active leak, broken lock), we prioritize and often come out same day. Text or call and we'll tell you exactly when we can be there.
Do you work in my area?
+
We're based in Simi Valley and serve the entire San Fernando Valley and surrounding areas — Simi Valley, Thousand Oaks, Chatsworth, Canoga Park, Woodland Hills, West Hills, Calabasas, Agoura Hills, North Hollywood, Burbank, and more. If you're within ~30 miles, reach out.
Do you work on weekends?
+
Yes. We work Monday through Saturday. For urgent jobs, we're reachable on Sundays — just text us and we'll figure it out.
