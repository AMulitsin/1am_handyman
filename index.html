📌 How Our Pricing Works

All prices assume standard conditions — replacing old with new, no custom modifications or cabinetry work required. We calculate a flat rate based on $60/hr, using our experience to estimate the job. Thanks to professional tools and expertise, we often finish faster than planned — but the price stays the same. If something unexpected comes up and takes longer, we don't charge extra. You pay for skill and speed, not the clock. For larger or complex projects, we provide a free estimate.
