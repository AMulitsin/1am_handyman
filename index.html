📌 How Our Pricing Works

All prices assume standard conditions — replacing old with new, no custom modifications or cabinetry work required. We calculate a flat rate based on $60/hr, using our experience to estimate the job. Thanks to professional tools and expertise, we often finish faster than planned — but the price stays the same. If something unexpected comes up and takes longer, we don't charge extra. You pay for skill and speed, not the clock. For larger or complex projects, we provide a free estimate.

Got Questions?

FAQ

How much does it cost just to come out and look?
+
Nothing. The estimate visit is completely free with no obligation. I come out, assess the job, and give you a clear price — you decide if you want to move forward. No pressure, no charge for the visit.
What if the job ends up being harder than expected?
+
If something unexpected comes up after we start, I stop and talk to you first — always. I don't add charges without your knowledge or approval. You agreed to a price, and I respect that. If the extra work is minor, I typically absorb it. If it's significant, we discuss it openly before continuing.
Do I need to buy materials, or do you handle that?
+
Both options work. For small items (outlets, fixtures, hardware), I typically carry common parts. For larger items or specific finishes, I'll give you a recommendation on exactly what to buy — or I can source it for you at cost, no markup. My goal is the most practical and affordable solution for you.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
+
Simple — I come back and make it right at no charge. I offer a 30-day workmanship guarantee on all jobs. My reputation is built on long-term relationships, not one-off transactions. If something isn't right, that matters to me personally.
How quickly can you come out?
+
For most jobs I can do a free estimate within 24–48 hours. For urgent situations (active leak, electrical issue, broken lock), I prioritize and often come out same day or next morning. Text or call and I'll tell you exactly when I can be there.
Do you work in my area?
+
I'm based in Simi Valley and serve the entire San Fernando Valley and surrounding areas — Simi Valley, Thousand Oaks, Chatsworth, Canoga Park, Woodland Hills, West Hills, Calabasas, Agoura Hills, North Hollywood, Burbank, and more. If you're within ~30 miles, reach out and I'll let you know.
Do you work on weekends?
+
Yes. I work Monday through Saturday. For urgent jobs, I'm reachable on Sundays as well — just text me and we'll figure it out.
