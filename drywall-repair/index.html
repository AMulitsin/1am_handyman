FAQ — Drywall Repair

Can you match my wall texture?
+
Yes. We match orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, popcorn, and smooth finishes. We test on a small area first to make sure it blends before finishing.
How long until I can paint?
+
Joint compound typically needs 24–48 hours to dry fully before painting, depending on temperature and humidity. We prime the patch so it accepts paint evenly.
Do I need to buy materials?
+
We carry common drywall materials and tools. For large jobs we'll specify what's needed. Materials are charged at cost — no markup.
What if the damage is from a leak?
+
We fix drywall after the leak is resolved. If you're not sure the leak is fixed, we can recommend what to check before we patch — we don't want to repair something that will get wet again.
