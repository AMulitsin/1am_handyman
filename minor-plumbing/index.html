FAQ — Minor Plumbing Repairs

Do I need to shut off the water before you arrive?
+
No — we handle that. We locate the shut-off for the specific fixture and work from there. No need to shut off the whole house.
Are parts included in the price?
+
Common small parts (supply lines, P-traps, flappers) are often included. For fixtures like faucets and toilets, we recommend you purchase the fixture of your choice and we install it.
What counts as minor plumbing?
+
Minor plumbing is fixture-level work — replacing or repairing items already connected to existing supply and drain lines. We do not re-route pipes or do rough-in plumbing, which requires a licensed contractor.
Is a permit required?
+
Most fixture replacements (faucets, toilets, disposals) do not require a permit in California. Larger scope work may — we'll advise you if that's the case for your project.
