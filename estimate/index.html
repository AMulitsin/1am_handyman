About Our Pricing

Are these prices guaranteed?
+
No. Every estimate shown here is a starting guide only. Final pricing is always confirmed after a free on-site evaluation. We never start work without your written or verbal approval of the price.
What affects the final price?
+
Scope of work, how easy the area is to access, existing conditions (is there blocking in the wall? Is there water damage?), materials required, and project complexity. Two jobs that sound the same can be very different in practice — that's why we always do a free estimate first.
How does the multi-unit discount work?
+
When you need the same service more than once in the same visit (for example, 3 outlets replaced, or 4 pictures hung), each additional unit gets a 10% discount — 2nd unit is 10% off, 3rd is 20% off, up to a maximum of 40% off. The first unit is always full price.
Is the free estimate really free?
+
Yes, completely. We come out, assess the job, give you a clear price. No obligation, no pressure. You decide if you want to move forward.
What is the minimum charge?
+
$120. This covers any service call and the first hour of work. Even the simplest jobs have a $120 minimum because we're coming to you, bringing professional tools, and doing the job right.
