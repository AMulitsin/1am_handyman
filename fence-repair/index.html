Got Questions?

FAQ — Fence & Gate Repair

Can you repair just part of my fence?
+
Yes — we repair or replace individual boards, posts, and sections without touching the rest of the fence. Spot repairs are much cheaper than full replacement.
What type of wood do you use?
+
We use pressure-treated lumber for posts (ground contact rated) and cedar or pressure-treated pine for boards, depending on your existing fence. We match what you have.
My gate keeps sagging — can that be fixed?
+
Usually yes. Most sagging gates can be fixed by replacing hardware, adding a tension rod or cable brace, or re-hanging the gate on a reinforced post.
Do I need an HOA approval for fence repairs?
+
Repairs to match existing fencing generally don't require HOA approval. If you're changing height, style, or color, check with your HOA first — we're happy to advise on what to ask.
