Got Questions?

FAQ — General Handyman

Can I give you a list of multiple tasks?
+
Absolutely — it's the most efficient use of our time and your money. Send the full list and we estimate time for each task.
What's the minimum charge?
+
$120 — the minimum for any visit. This covers the first hour. At $70/hr, the first hour of actual work included in the minimum.
Do you bring your own tools?
+
Yes — drills, levels, saws, all common anchors and fasteners. We rarely need a hardware store run mid-job.
What if you can't do something on my list?
+
We tell you upfront, before starting, if something is outside our scope. We'll tell you what type of contractor you need.
Do you do one-time visits or regular maintenance?
+
Both. Many clients use us for periodic maintenance visits — quarterly or seasonally. Regular clients get priority scheduling.
What areas do you cover?
+
Simi Valley and a 30-mile radius: Thousand Oaks, Moorpark, Chatsworth, West Hills, Canoga Park, Woodland Hills, Calabasas, North Hollywood, Burbank, and more.
