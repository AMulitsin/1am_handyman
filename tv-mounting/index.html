Got Questions?

FAQ — TV Mounting

How long does TV mounting take?
+
Most installations take 30–90 minutes depending on mount type, cable routing, and wall material. We're in and out quickly.
Do you hide the cables in the wall?
+
Yes — we offer in-wall cable routing for a fully clean look. We can also use surface cable channels if in-wall isn't possible (concrete walls, rental apartments). Both options look professional.
Do I need to supply the mount?
+
You can supply your own mount, or we can recommend and source the right one for your TV size and viewing angle — at cost, no markup.
Can you mount above a fireplace?
+
Yes. We can mount above fireplaces including brick and stone surfaces. We'll advise on the best anchoring method and ensure the mount is level and secure.
