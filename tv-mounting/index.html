Got Questions?

FAQ — TV Mounting

How long does TV mounting take?
+
Most jobs take 30–90 minutes. A simple flat mount on drywall is fast; full-motion on concrete or in-wall cable routing takes longer. We'll give you a realistic window when you book.
Do you hide the cables?
+
Yes. We route cables inside the wall for a fully clean look, or use a surface raceway channel if in-wall isn't possible (concrete, rental units). Both options look professional.
What if there are no studs where I want the TV?
+
We use toggle anchors rated for the TV's weight. We never mount on bare drywall without proper anchoring — safety is non-negotiable.
Do I supply the mount?
+
You can buy your own or we can recommend and source the right one. We carry common mounts for same-day jobs.
Can you mount above a fireplace?
+
Yes — brick, stone, tile, or drywall above a fireplace. We advise on heat exposure and viewing angle for above-fireplace positions.
What about very large TVs (75"+)?
+
85" and larger TVs require a full-motion or very sturdy fixed mount. We always recommend two-person install for 75"+, which is included in our pricing.
