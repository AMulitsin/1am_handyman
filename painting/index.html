Got Questions?

FAQ — Painting

Do you supply the paint?
+
You can supply paint in your chosen color and finish, or we can recommend what to buy. We carry brushes, rollers, tape, and drop cloths.
How many coats do you apply?
+
Typically two coats for walls changing color, one coat for touch-ups on the same color. We always prime over repairs and bare drywall first.
How long does painting a room take?
+
A standard bedroom takes 4–6 hours including prep and cleanup. Larger rooms or rooms with trim take longer. We'll give you a realistic time estimate upfront.
Do you move furniture?
+
We move lightweight furniture away from walls and cover everything with drop cloths. Large items like sofas and beds should be moved or cleared first if possible.
